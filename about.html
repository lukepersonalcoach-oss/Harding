About

Luke Harding

I bring over 20 years' classroom experience, including a decade in senior leadership focused on professional learning.

Why I Do This

After years of delivering and evaluating CPD, I grew frustrated with training that felt unambitious, incoherent and disconnected from the reality of schools. Too often, courses amount to a disparate collection of tools and models, aiming for participant satisfaction rather than meaningful professional growth.

I wanted to create something more thoughtful, more challenging, and more human.

I pursued this through an MBA focused on the school as a learning organisation, the barriers to professional learning, building effective middle leadership teams, and leadership socialisation — combining rigorous research with two decades of practice at the chalkface and in senior leadership.

Background & Credentials

Most recently, I was Assistant Head (Learning) at Aldenham School, a wide-ranging senior leadership role spanning professional learning, teacher development, SEN and EAL provision. I left this role to establish this consultancy, so I could bring that experience directly to other schools. I have served as Head of Sixth Form, Acting SENDCO, and Director of English and Head of House.

I hold a First Class degree in English Language and Literature from Oxford University, an MBA in Educational Leadership (Distinction) and NPQSL from UCL. I am a trained Deputy Designated Safeguarding Lead, a member of the Chartered College of Teaching, and have examined for King's College London's MA in Educational Leadership.
